UIW Team Project Progress

How to read this and move forward. Your ring is overall completion (closed issues, milestones, and project board Done items). The amber tick on the pace bar is where the class is expected to be this week. Green means on pace, amber means 15 points or more behind, red means 30 or more behind. Click your row in the member table: it lists your personal warnings and exactly what to fix. Linked is the share of your commits whose message references an issue (#N); keep it high so your work maps to the board. Flags fire on the patterns that cost real points at grading time: commits with no issue reference, activity bunched into one short burst, one-line or text-only commits claiming finished work, and creating no issues. Your repository is the record of your effort: commit real artifacts (configs, scripts, screenshots, logs, wiki pages), steadily, all semester. Data refreshes weekly and whenever the registry changes.
